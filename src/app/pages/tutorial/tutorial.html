Welcome to Plusultra Sales Application

The sales application is a practical preview of the sales and marketing in action, and a demonstration of our technology.

What is this Sales Application?

Plusultra Sales Application is an open source sales and marketing planning applicaiton that enables a sales and marketing teams to build high efficient activities and managements. The teams can expect maximum profits.

How this Sales Application works?

Information is a powerful tool of sales and marketing. Therefore, this application will make it handy in your smartphone through a shared online Cloud storage such as Google Drive or iCloud. One sales and marketing team can share their sales, customer, congress, location and daily updates.
